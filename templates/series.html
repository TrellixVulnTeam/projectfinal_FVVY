How to recognize series circuit

When connected in series, resistors form a string in which there is only one path for current.

  • The current is the same through all points in a series circuit.
  • When resitors are connected in series, the resistor values add because each resitor offers opposition to the current in direct proportion to its resistance. $$R_t = R_1 + R_2 + R_3 + ... + R_n $$
  • The reciprocal of resistance is conductance, symbolized by G. it is measure of the ease with with which current is established. Formula : $$ G = {1 \over R }$$
